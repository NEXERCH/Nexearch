## What Is NEXERCH

NEXERCH is an independent research organization building systems that operate reliably under uncertainty — in space, in safety-critical environments, and at the frontier of autonomous intelligence.

We exist to prove one thing: **independent research can compete with institutions.**

We publish systems. We publish standards. We publish research. Others adopt them.

---

## Standards

NEXERCH develops open standards for autonomous systems, formal verification, and safety-critical architectures. These standards are designed to be adopted by researchers, aerospace organizations, and industry — independent of institutional affiliation.

| Standard | Domain | Status |

| ATLAS Flight Software Architecture | Reusable Launch Vehicles | Published |
| K-Framework Uncertainty Protocol | Space Domain Awareness | Published |
| Multi-Agent Authority Standard | Distributed Autonomous Systems | Planned |

## Community

NEXERCH is a global community of independent researchers, engineers, and builders.

We do not gate contributions behind institutional affiliation, geography, or credentials.
We gate them behind **quality, depth, and intellectual honesty.**

### Who We Work With

- Researchers working independently outside traditional institutions
- Engineers building safety-critical and autonomous systems
- Organizations adopting NEXERCH standards in their work
- Anyone who thinks in systems, not just in code

### How to Contribute

| Path | Description |
|---|---|
| **Founding Member** | Core architects shaping NEXERCH systems and standards |
| **Research Contributor** | Domain specialists contributing to specific systems |
| **Standards Adopter** | Organizations implementing NEXERCH standards |
| **Community Contributor** | Reviewers, documenters, and domain advisors |

To join: reach out with your work. No CV required. No institution required.

## Governance

NEXERCH operates as an independent research organization with no institutional parent.

- Decisions are made by founding members
- Standards are published openly under Apache 2.0 or CC-BY 4.0
- All research is archived with permanent DOIs via Zenodo
- No research is paywalled. Ever.
